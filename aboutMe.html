About me

I live in the city of Odessa, Ukraine. I have a great desire to get into a team of developers with a good code culture, join the development industry and get the right start in programming. I’m looking for a job as a Trainee or a Junior Frontend Developer. I'm looking for a full time job both in the office and remote work.

I have a master's degree in building engineering design. For 9 years I worked as an engineer in the construction industry, where I gained practical experience in the implementation of large construction projects. My responsibilities were:

  • Development of project documentation for residential complexes, industrial and commercial facilities.
  • Leading a team of design engineers.
  • Communication with clients, designers, contractors, allied partners.
  • Collection of initial data, development of an engineering concept, preparation and coordination of technical tasks.
  • Verification of documentation for compliance with the current regulations of Ukraine and the requirements of the company.
  • Linking all engineering sections to each other
  • Control over the issuance of project documentation according to schedules.
